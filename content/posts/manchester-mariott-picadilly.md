The Marriott Manchester Piccadilly is a hotel in Manchester that is almost impossible to get to by car. 


The shower it's self is fairly inoffensive and clearly modern.
It was almost the perfect shower, with great pressure, perfect temperature and a choice of both a handheld and overhead shower head.
However, the mechanism was frustrating. None of the knobs were labeled, leaving you naked and afraid trying to and figure it out.
To add insult to injury, the positioning of the shower door means that if you make the wrong move whilst trying to make sense of it you are blasted with cold water.
