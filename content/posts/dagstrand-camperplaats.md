This is a very cheap camp site next to a very nice lake. There is a cafe, but it's only open for 2 hours per day 3 days a week.

There is a single shower, it is a normal glass shower cubicle that you might find in someones home, only it's sat outside on a pallet next to a temporary toilet block.
There are two modes: on and off. There is one temperature (cold) and one pressure (hard) and everyone can see you whilst you're showering.
