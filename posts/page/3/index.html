This isn’t a parking review, though, so I’m going to ignore that. The hotel seems to have been built around 2016, making it at most 6 years old. Which is why it’s no surprise that the showers in this place are immaculate. The surfaces were so pristine I felt like I was the first person to ever step foot into that shower. The latest in shower technology has been employed here, with an immaculate thermostatic mixer shower with a detachable head.
